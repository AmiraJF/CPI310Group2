About the Project

Our projects goal is to help create study groups in the world of online classes. Due to the coronavirus, classes have gone online, because of this many students are struggling to meet people and create these study groups. We plan to help fix this by:

  1. Listing classes and allowing the students to join those classes.
  2. Having boards to ask questions for certain classes
  3. Allow them to set up different study groups for each class

We felt that this is neccessary because it is much harder to meet people without the in person interaction we normally would get in class.
